Welcome

Discover a whole new way you can treat yourself to high quality education and training!  FC is the institution you can trust to provide education and training programmes designed especially with you in mind.

Fortcode College offers co-education within a balanced equity profile, preparing and enabling students to fully integrate and interact with
the broad spectrum of cultural diversity in today’s world.

Mission Statement

Our primary mission is to provide programs and services leading to certificates and career skills needed for success in the workplace.

Our Purpose

To provide opportunities for academic excellence, student success, vocational education, and fostering community partnerships, and stewardship

Education

We equip young people with the knowledge and opportunity for them to make informed decisions about their future. Decisions that are based on their individual ambitions and talents.

Knowledge and
Opportunity

Fortcode College is an upmarket academic, vocational and skills centre for Forms 1-6 located in the lush, quiet surroundings on 9 Wretham Road, Mount Pleasant which is registered with the Ministry of Primary and Secondary Education as well as the Ministry of Higher and Tertiary Education.

Our classes are small to cater for the different abilities of our students

How we help Our Students

We are passionate about the fact that all young people should be able to experience the mix of academic and vocational learning and that from an early young age, they are aware that there are many paths to success.
